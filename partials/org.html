Agoram Sivaramakrishnan

Our Honourable Guru

Pandit Sivaramakrishnan had his Adhyanam from two eminent organizations: H.H. Sri Kanchi Kamakoti Shri Mahaswami Vidya Peeta Trust at Sivasthanam in Thenambakkam, Kanchipuram and Thiruvidaimarthur Prayoga Pathasala attached to the Kanchi Sankara Mutt. Currently, he is the Chief Priest at the Sanatan Dharma Temple.
